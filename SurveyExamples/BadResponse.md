## Bad Survey Response Example

**Context:**  
In the survey, participants were shown short stories designed to implicitly convey the meaning of specific idioms.  
They were asked to interpret each story and associate it with an idiom.  
This example illustrates a response that is formally complete but fails to provide usable content.

---

**Respondent**
- Country: United Kingdom  
- Native language: English  
- English proficiency: Native  
- Task difficulty: Very hard  

---

### Idioms Tested
- **Story 1:** *The grass is always greener (on the other side)*  
- **Story 2:** *The elephant in the room*  
- **Story 3:** *Don’t count your chickens before they hatch*

### Idiom Knowledge

### Participant Feedback

---

### Why this is a **bad response**
- No narrative or situational content  
- Explicit refusal or inability to respond  
- All idiom knowledge marked as “don’t know”  
- Cannot be meaningfully evaluated or matched
