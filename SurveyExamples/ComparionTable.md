## Good vs Bad Survey Responses — Comparison

| Aspect | Good Response | Bad Response |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| Completion status | COMPLETE | COMPLETE |
| Narrative content | Clear, coherent stories | No narrative (“don’t know”, “can’t think”) |
| Length | Multi-sentence | Empty / one-liner |
| Idiom meaning | Expressed implicitly through situation | Not expressed |
| Engagement | High (attempts all stories) | Very low |
| Idiom knowledge | Partial / full | All marked “don’t know” |
| Evaluatable | Yes | No |
| Usable for analysis | Yes | Discarded / filtered |
